About Us:

SuperStudying was founded in 2019 during the pandemic to help people Study. It was a hard time because we were at home. So I started SuperStudying! I plan to update it each year. And i hope others help too! SuperStudying was Founded by Ethan C. We now have a large team of Studiers that help each other. We also have a large team of helpers that can help you with whatever you need!
